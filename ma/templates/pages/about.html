Who we are


My Allowance is an online chore and allowance app with a reward system that teaches kids the basic financial principles surrounding earning, saving, sharing and spending money wisely

Our Story


My allowance team came together in 2017 to bring innovation to the somewhat stagnated education sector and growing financial sector in Uganda. Globalization and digitalization have made learning more accessible and efficient than ever before, we hope to inspire educators and technologists rethink ways of impacting youths and children.

Mission


Empowering children to socially and economically empower themselves for the future.
